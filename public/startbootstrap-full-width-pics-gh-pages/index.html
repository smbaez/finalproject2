Settling Up

Searching for a future home has never been easier!



Mission Statement:
We’re seeking to build a user interface that helps young professionals narrow down possible neighborhoods that they can call home and build a career at - based on preferences that are unique to them.

You might know which city you will soon call home, but what about the neighborhood you'll live in? Beyond common considerations like rent and commute, many young professionals care about specific factors that defined their college experience and their personalities. We will be integrating real-time data collected by city governments, as opposed to relying on comment sections of blogs and websites recommended by others. For this project, we’ll focus on New York City, specifically the borough of Brooklyn - one of the most sought after boroughs for new grads.
